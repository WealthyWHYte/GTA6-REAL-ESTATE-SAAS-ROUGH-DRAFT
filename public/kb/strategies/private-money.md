# Private Money Loan Strategy

## Overview
Financing from private investors (not institutions) who lend their personal capital. More flexible than hard money with better terms.

## When to Use
- Better terms than hard money
- Long-term hold scenarios
- Repeat investor relationships
- Bridge financing
- Vacation/secondary properties

## Key Terms
- **Interest Rate**: 8-12% annual
- **Points**: 1-2%
- **Term**: 1-5 years
- **LTV**: Up to 80% of ARV
- **Personal Guarantees**: Usually required

## Pros
- ✅ Better rates than hard money
- ✅ Flexible terms
- ✅ Relationship-based
- ✅ Can negotiate
- ✅ May report to credit

## Cons
- ❌ Requires relationship
- ❌ Personal guarantee needed
- ❌ Limited availability
- ❌ Due on sale clause

## Typical Deal
- Loan Amount: $400,000
- Interest: 10% ($3,333/month)
- Points: 1% ($4,000)
- Term: 3 years
- LTV: 75%

## Finding Private Money
1. Real estate investor groups
2. Friends and family
3. Self-directed IRA
4. Syndication groups
5. Note investors

## Exit Strategies
1. Traditional refinance
2. Sell property
3. Cash-out refi
4. Extend/renew loan
