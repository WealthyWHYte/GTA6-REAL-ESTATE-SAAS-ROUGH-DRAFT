# All Cash Purchase Strategy

## Overview
Pay full cash for properties with no financing contingency. This creates the strongest offers and fastest closings.

## When to Use
- Motivated sellers needing quick closes
- Auction properties
- Fixer-uppers where financing is difficult
- Properties needing work that won't qualify for traditional loans
- Competition with other investors

## Key Terms
- **Purchase Price**: Full asking price or negotiated
- **Earnest Money**: 1-2% of purchase price
- **Closing**: 7-21 days
- **Inspections**: For information only (AS-IS)

## Pros
- ✅ Fastest closing (7-14 days typical)
- ✅ No financing contingency
- ✅ Strongest offer in competitive situations
- ✅ No appraisal contingency
- ✅ Seller prefers (certainty)

## Cons
- ❌ Ties up capital
- ❌ No leverage
- ❌ Full market exposure if market drops
- ❌ Limited to available cash

## Typical Terms
- Down Payment: 100% (cash)
- Closing Costs: Buyer pays title insurance
- Inspection: Information only
- Possession: At closing

## Negotiation Script
"We can close in 7 days with all cash. No financing contingency, no appraisal contingency. We're prepared to close as-is with a $10,000 earnest money deposit. This eliminates all risk for you."

## Exit Strategies
1. Refinance after purchase (cash-out refi)
2. Sell to end buyer
3. Rent for cash flow
4. BRRRR strategy
