# Hard Money Loan Strategy

## Overview
Short-term financing from private lenders at high interest rates and points. Used for quick purchases, flips, and time-sensitive deals.

## When to Use
- Fix and flip projects
- Time-sensitive opportunities
- Bad credit / no traditional financing
- Bridge financing needed
- Quick close required (7-14 days)

## Key Terms
- **Interest Rate**: 12-18% annual
- **Points**: 2-4% of loan amount
- **Term**: 6-12 months
- **LTV**: Up to 70-75% of ARV

## Cost Structure
- $500,000 loan
- 15% interest = $75,000/year
- 3 points = $15,000 upfront
- Total first year cost: $90,000

## Pros
- ✅ Fast approval (24-72 hours)
- ✅ Flexible criteria
- ✅ Based on deal, not credit
- ✅ Close in 7-14 days
- ✅ Can fund quickly

## Cons
- ❌ High interest rates
- ❌ Points upfront
- ❌ Short term pressure
- ❌ Property must be solid
- ❌ Exit strategy required

## When to Use
- Flip with high ROI potential
- Buy below market
- Quick resale value
- Bridge to other financing

## Exit Strategies
1. Sell after rehab (flip)
2. Refinance to long-term
3. Cash-out refi
4. Sell to end buyer
