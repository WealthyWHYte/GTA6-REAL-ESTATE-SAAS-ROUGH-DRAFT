## 📊 The Math

### Revenue Formula
```
Gross Revenue = Contracts × Avg Contract Value × Frequency
```

### Profit Formula
```
Net Profit = Gross Revenue - Carrier Payouts - Platform Fee - Overhead

Break-Even Point = Fixed Costs ÷ Margin %

You need $13,333 in gross revenue to break even.
```

## 🎯 The Real Numbers

To hit **$100K/month**:

```
If your margin = 15%:
$100,000 ÷ 0.15 = $666,667 gross revenue needed

At $150/delivery:
666,667 ÷ 150 = 4,444 deliveries/month
4,444 ÷ 30 = 148 deliveries/day

You need ~150 deliveries/day
With 25 carriers = 6 deliveries/carrier/day

That's very achievable in a metro area.
```
