## 1. Purchase Price & Terms

**Purchase Price:** $[Full Asking Price]

**Structure:**
1. **Down Payment:** $[10% of Price] paid at closing
2. **Existing Financing (Subject-To)::** Buyer to take over payments on existing mortgage of approx. $[Balance] at [Rate]%
3. **Seller Carryback:** Seller to carry remaining equity of $[Gap Amount] at [2-5]% interest
4. **Payments:** Monthly P&I payments to Seller
5. **Balloon:** All remaining principal due in [120] months

---

## 2. Due Diligence & Closing

- **Inspection Period:** ⭐ **7-DAY INSPECTION PERIOD** - Buyer has 7 calendar days from mutual acceptance to inspect property and terminate agreement for ANY reason
- **Closing Date:** 30 days from end of inspection period
- **Title & Escrow:** Creative-finance-friendly title company or attorney
- **Financing Contingency:** Buyer may terminate within inspection period if unable to secure financing

---

## 3. Investor/Wholesaler Protections ⭐

### Assignability Clause:
> "Buyer reserves the right to assign this contract to an end buyer, investor, or wholesaler at any time prior to closing without Seller's consent. Original Buyer shall remain liable until closing."

### Inspection & Exit Rights:
> "Buyer has the absolute right to terminate this agreement within 7 calendar days of mutual acceptance for any reason, in Buyer's sole discretion. Upon termination, all earnest money deposits shall be immediately returned to Buyer."

### Equity & Condition Contingency:
> "This offer is contingent upon Buyer's verification of property condition, title, and financial viability. Buyer may terminate within the inspection period if inspections reveal material defects, liens, or conditions unsatisfactory to Buyer."

---

## 4. Disclosures & Risk Mitigation

- **Due-on-Sale Clause:** Buyer and Seller acknowledge the risk. Buyer will use third-party servicer.
- **Insurance:** Buyer named as "Additional Insured" on existing policy
- **Servicing:** All payments handled by licensed third-party servicer
- **Seller Disclosures:** Seller to disclose all known defects, liens, and encumbrances

---

## 5. Closing Costs

- **Buyer Pays:** Title insurance, escrow fees, transfer taxes
- **Seller Pays:** Existing loan payoffs, any liens
- **Proration:** Property taxes and HOA fees prorated at closing

---

## 6. Acceptance

This LOI is **non-binding** and is intended to outline basic terms for a formal Purchase and Sale Agreement (PSA).
