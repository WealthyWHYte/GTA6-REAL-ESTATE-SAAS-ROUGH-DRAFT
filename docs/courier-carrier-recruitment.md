## 💰 Phase 5: Payment Structure

### Standard Models

| Model | You Take | Carrier Gets | Best For |
|-------|----------|-------------|----------|
| Net 7 | 15% | 85% | Established |
| Net 15 | 12% | 88% | New carriers |
| Net 30 | 10% | 90% | Trusted |
| Flat per job | varies | fixed | Specific routes |

### Payment Terms to Define

```
• When do you pay? (weekly, daily, per job)
• How do you pay? (Venmo, Zelle, direct deposit)
• What's the minimum payout? ($100, $250)
• When are invoices submitted? (EOD, EOW)
• What's late policy? (10% penalty)
```

## 🔥 Pro Tips

### What Carriers Really Want

1. **Fast pay** - #1 factor
2. **Consistent work** - Not hunting daily
3. **Fair rates** - Don't skim too much
4. **Respect** - Good communication
5. **Support** - Dispatch backing

### Red Flags to Avoid

- Won't provide insurance
- Can't pass background check
- Only available sporadically
- Poor communication
- History of claims

### Building Loyalty

- Pay on time, every time
- Give first choice to top performers
- Provide volume during slow periods
- Give advance notice of contracts
- Resolve issues quickly

### Carrier Questions Answered

**Q: How do I get paid?**
A: Weekly on Fridays via Zelle or direct deposit.

A: No penalty, but consistent rejections may affect priority.

**Q: Can I work with other dispatchers?**

**Q: What happens if I damage cargo?**
