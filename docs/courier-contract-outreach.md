## 📞 Phase 2: Cold Calling Script

### The Opener

```
"Hi [Name], my name is [Your Name] and I'm with GTA6 Courier Network. 
I'm reaching out because we help companies like yours with their delivery needs.
Do you have 30 seconds for me to tell you what we do?"
```

### If They Say Yes:

```
"[Company] handles [their industry] which typically has daily courier needs.
We specialize in [time-sensitive/high-value] deliveries and work with 
companies like [similar company] in [area].

I'm curious - how are you handling your deliveries right now?"
```

### If They Say They Have a Courier:

```
"That's great, most companies do. We're actually taking on new clients 
for our overflow capacity right now. 

One thing that makes us different - we can typically beat turnaround time 
by 30% because we have dedicated drivers in the area.

Would it make sense to set up a quick 5-minute call so I can show you 
what our capabilities are?"
```

### If Interested - Close:

```
"Perfect. Here's what I'd suggest - let's do a trial run with your next 
rush job. We'll show you the quality, and if you like it, we can discuss 
ongoing rates. 

What does your schedule look like this week for a quick onboarding call?"
```

## 🔥 Pro Tips

### What Works

1. **Lead with value, not price**
   - Focus on reliability, speed, service
   
2. **Use social proof**
   - "We handle deliveries for [similar company]"

3. **Offer trials**
   - "Try us on one job, see the difference"

4. **Follow up relentlessly**
   - Most sales lost at 3+ contacts

5. **Get referrals**
   - "Do you know other companies that might need this?"

### What Doesn't Work

1. **Leading with price**
2. **Being pushy**
3. **Giving up after 1 call**
4. **Not understanding their needs**
5. **Not having clear differentiators**
