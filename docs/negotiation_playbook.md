# Negotiation Playbook
## Creative Finance Offer Strategy

---

## Core Philosophy

**Creative finance is not about price — it's about structure.**

Our goal is to manufacture superior debt, not to negotiate the lowest price. A slightly higher price with favorable terms often beats a lower price with standard terms.

---

## The Offer Positioning Strategy

### Step 1: Calculate Your Walk-Away Number (WAN)

This is your ceiling — the highest price where the numbers still work:

```
WAN = Maximum price where:
- Cash flow ≥ $500/month
- Equity at Year 7 ≥ 25%
- Total Entry ≤ 15% of value
```

### Step 2: Open at 75-80% of WAN

**Why?**
- Expect a counter → they come up, you meet in middle
- Middle of 80% and 100% = 90% of WAN → still favorable
- If they accept 80% → extraordinary deal

### Step 3: Structure Wins at Any Price

Even at lower prices, maintain:
- 40-year amortization (crushes payment)
- 5-7% interest cap
- 10% or less down
- 7-10 year balloon

## Offer Types & When to Use

### 1. Cash Anchor Offer
**Use when:** Seller is motivated, property on market >60 days

```
Price: 80-85% of value
Terms: Close in 21 days, As-Is
```

### 2. Terms Offer (Primary)
**Use when:** Most situations, seller has equity

```
Price: 92-98% of value
Down: 5-10%
Seller carries financing OR assumes existing mortgage
Close: 21-30 days
```

### 3. Subject-To Offer
**Use when:** Existing rate is significantly below market (< 6.5%)

```
Price: 90-95% of value
Take over existing mortgage (no new loan)
Seller carries second if equity > 30%
```

### 4. Seller Financing Offer
**Use when:** Property is free-and-clear or high equity

```
Price: 95-100% of value
You carry first position
5-10% down, you finance remainder
Rate: 5-7%, 40-year amort, 7-10 year balloon
```

---

## Rate vs. Price Trade Rule

**Critical:** Never argue about rate. Trade rate for price.

### Formula
For every 1% increase in rate above 5%, reduce offer price by 2-3%.

### Example
| Seller Will Accept | Your Offer Adjustment |
|--------------------|-----------------------|
| 5% rate | Full price (100%) |
| 6% rate | Reduce by 2-3% |
| 7% rate | Reduce by 4-6% |
| 8% rate | Reduce by 6-9% |

### Response Template
> "If the seller needs a higher rate, we'd need to adjust the price to maintain the same payment profile. What works best for them?"

## Counter Offer Strategy

### When They Counter Higher

**Step 1:** Calculate new payment
**Step 2:** Check if still passes cash flow test ($500+)
**Step 3:** Respond with one of these:

#### Option A: Meet Halfway
```
Offer = (Your offer + Their counter) / 2
```
Best for: Strong properties you want

#### Option B: Hold Your Ground
```
"We appreciate the counter. Our offer represents our best 
position given our investment criteria. If this doesn't work, 
we wish you well."
```
Best for: Commodity properties, high days on market

#### Option C: Trade Terms
```
"We can increase price if seller agrees to:
- Carry 5% second at 0% interest
- Or: 45-day close instead of 30"
```
Best for: Creating value through structure

## Objection Handling Matrix

| Objection | Response Strategy |
|-----------|-------------------|
| "Price too low" | Explain underwriting, offer to discuss terms |
| "Multiple offers" | Reinforce strengths, offer proof of funds |
| "Want cash" | Explain financing is equally strong |
| "Need more time" | Offer extension with small escalation |
| "Seller wants full price" | Pivot to terms discussion |

## The Negotiation Flow

```
INITIAL OFFER (80% of WAN)
        ↓
    RESPONSE?
        ↓
   [No Response] → Follow-up Day 3, 7, 14, 30
        ↓
   [Counter] → Calculate new payment → Test cash flow
        ↓
   [Accept] → Send acceptance, wire earnest money
        ↓
   [Reject] → Thank them, ask to stay in touch
```

---

## Key Principles

1. **Never get emotional** — Pure math, no ego
2. **Always protect cash flow** — $500 minimum
3. **Trade, don't argue** — Rate for price, terms for price
4. **Volume wins** — Accept 1-3% acceptance rate
5. **Walk away cleanly** — "Best of luck" maintains relationships
6. **Document everything** — Build your knowledge base

## Closing Techniques

### When Terms Agreed:

> "Excellent. We'll wire earnest money within 24 hours. Please send the contract for review. Looking forward to closing."

### When They Reject:

> "We understand. This property didn't fit our criteria, but we appreciate being considered. Please keep us in mind for future opportunities. Best of luck."

### When They Go Silent:

> "I understand you're busy. We'll plan to follow up in a week. If anything changes, we're still interested and can move quickly."
