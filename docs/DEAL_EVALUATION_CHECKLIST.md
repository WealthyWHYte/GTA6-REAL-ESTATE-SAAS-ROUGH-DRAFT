# Deal Evaluation Checklist: Hybrid Creative Finance Structures

Use this checklist to evaluate $1M+ properties for Subject-To, Seller Finance, or Hybrid potential.

---

## 1. Property Data & Motivation

- [ ] **Days on Market (DOM):** Is property >60 days? (Higher = Higher Motivation)
- [ ] **Listing Status:** Active or Price Reduced?
- [ ] **Owner Type:** Individual, Trust, or Company? (Trusts/Individuals more flexible)
- [ ] **Owner Occupied:** Non-owner occupied/Vacant = easier to negotiate

---

## 2. Financial Underwriting (The Sub-To Check)

- [ ] **Existing Mortgage Balance:** Current balance?
- [ ] **Existing Interest Rate:** <4.5%? (If yes, prioritize Subject-To)
- [ ] **Mortgage Type:** Conventional, FHA, or VA?
- [ ] **Equity Position:**
  - [ ] **Low Equity (<20%):** Level 1 (Sub-To + Small Cash to Seller)
  - [ ] **Medium Equity (20-50%):** Level 2 (Hybrid)
  - [ ] **High Equity (>50%):** Level 3 (Seller Finance)

---

## 3. The Hybrid Structure (Sub-To + Seller Carryback)

- [ ] **The Gap:** Asking Price - Existing Mortgage = Gap Amount
- [ ] **Seller Carryback Potential:** Will seller carry the gap as 2nd lien?
- [ ] **Interest Rate on Carryback:** Can we get <4%?
- [ ] **Down Payment:** Total cash to close <10% of purchase price?

---

## 4. Risk Mitigation & Compliance

- [ ] **Due-on-Sale Clause:** Disclosed to seller?
- [ ] **Insurance:** Can buyer be named "Additional Insured"?
- [ ] **Servicing:** Third-party servicer (Westcor, Allied)?
- [ ] **Title:** Creative-finance-friendly title company/attorney?

---

## 4B. Investor/Wholesaler Protections ⭐

### Must-Have Contract Clauses:
- [ ] **7-Day Inspection Period:** Either party can terminate within 7 days for ANY reason
- [ ] **Assignability Clause:** Buyer can assign/ wholesale to end buyer without seller consent
- [ ] **Financing Contingency:** Exit if can't secure financing
- [ ] **Earnest Money Protection:** Deposit refunded if buyer exits during inspection
- [ ] **Title Contingency:** Exit if title issues discovered
- [ ] **Property Condition Contingency:** Exit if inspections reveal defects
- [ ] **No Personal Liability After Assignment:** Original buyer released after assignment

### Contract Language Templates:

**7-Day Exit Clause:**
> "Buyer has the absolute right to terminate this Agreement within Seven (7) calendar days of mutual acceptance for any reason, in Buyer's sole discretion. Upon termination, all earnest money deposits shall be immediately returned to Buyer."

**Wholesale/Assignment Clause:**
> "Buyer reserves the right to assign this contract to any third party, investor, or wholesaler at any time prior to closing without Seller's consent. Original Buyer shall remain liable until closing, at which point all liability transfers to assignee."

**No Contingency (Cash Offer):**
> "This offer is a CASH offer with no financing contingency. Buyer has 7 days to verify property condition and title. Buyer may terminate for material defects only."

---

## 5. Market Analysis Validation

- [ ] **Rental Yield:** Does projected rent cover total debt service?
- [ ] **DSCR:** >1.2?
- [ ] **Exit Strategy:**
  - [ ] **Plan A:** Long-term rental (Cash flow)
  - [ ] **Plan B:** Refinance in 5-7 years
  - [ ] **Plan C:** Resell on terms (Wrap mortgage)

---

## Scoring Guide

### Motivation Score (0-30 pts)
- DOM >90 days: +15 pts
- DOM 60-90 days: +10 pts
- DOM 30-60 days: +5 pts
- Price reduced: +10 pts
- Vacant/Non-owner: +5 pts

### Equity Position (0-25 pts)
- Free & Clear: Seller Finance ONLY
- High Equity (>50%): Seller Finance or Hybrid
- Medium Equity (20-50%): Hybrid
- Low Equity (<20%): Subject-To

### Existing Mortgage (0-20 pts)
- Rate <4.5%: +20 pts (KEEP THIS RATE!)
- Rate 4.5-6%: +10 pts
- Rate >6%: +0 pts
- No mortgage: Seller Finance path

### Cash Flow (0-15 pts)
- DSCR >1.3: +15 pts
- DSCR 1.2-1.3: +10 pts
- DSCR 1.1-1.2: +5 pts
- DSCR <1.1: REJECT

### Market Potential (0-10 pts)
- Tier 1 Market: +10 pts
- Tier 2 Market: +7 pts
- Tier 3 Market: +5 pts
