# Market Analysis Framework: Validating Creative Finance Offers

This framework provides the methodology for performing market analysis to validate creative finance offers for $1M+ properties across the US.

## 1. Market Selection & Demand Analysis

### Demand Indicators
- **Population Growth:** Target >1% annual growth
- **Job Market:** Major employers (Tech, Medical, Military)
- **Rental Demand:** >90% occupancy for LTR, >60% for STR

### Market Tiers
- **Tier 1 (High Yield):** Miami, FL; Austin, TX; Phoenix, AZ
- **Tier 2 (Stable Cash Flow):** Augusta, GA; Grovetown, GA; Charlotte, NC
- **Tier 3 (Equity Plays):** Los Angeles, CA; San Francisco, CA; New York, NY

## 2. Rental Yield Validation

### LTR Analysis
- **Market Rent:** Use Zillow, Rentometer, Apartments.com
- **DSCR Formula:** NOI / Total Debt Service
- **Target:** DSCR >1.2

### STR Analysis
- **ADR:** Use AirDNA or Rabbu
- **Occupancy:** Target >55%
- **Yield:** 2x-3x higher than LTR

## 3. Equity & Appreciation Analysis

### Appreciation Potential
- Historical Growth: >5% annually over 10 years
- Future Catalysts: New developments, transit, corporate HQs

### Equity Capture
- Discount to Market: Purchase at or below Estimated Value
- Forced Appreciation: Add value (ADU) to increase >20%

## 4. Offer Validation Matrix

| Metric | Level 1 (Sub-To) | Level 3 (Seller Finance) |
|--------|------------------|--------------------------|
| DSCR Target | >1.1 | >1.3 |
| Cash-on-Cash Return | >15% | >10% |
| Equity Capture | >10% | >5% |
| Exit Strategy | Refinance 5-7 years | Hold 10+ years |

## 5. Conclusion

Every creative finance offer must be:
1. Financially sound (DSCR, Cash-on-Cash)
2. Market-validated (rental demand, appreciation)
3. Structurally sound (exit strategy defined)
