# Creative Finance Underwriting & Offer Logic Guide

This guide provides a detailed framework for an AI Underwriter to generate optimal offers for $1M+ properties using Subject-To, Seller Finance, and Hybrid creative finance strategies.

## 1. Understanding Seller Motivation & Property Equity

**Seller Motivation Indicators:**
- **Days on Market (DOM):** Properties with high DOM (>60 days) often indicate a motivated seller willing to consider creative terms.
- **Listing Status:** "Price Reduced" or "Expired" listings are strong indicators of motivation.
- **Owner Type:** Individual or Trust owners are generally more flexible than corporate entities.
- **Owner Occupancy:** Non-owner occupied or vacant properties suggest a higher likelihood of a seller wanting to offload the asset quickly.

**Property Equity Position:**
- **High Equity (>50%):** Ideal for pure Seller Finance or a Hybrid structure where the seller carries a significant portion of the purchase price.
- **Medium Equity (20-50%):** Suitable for Hybrid deals, combining Subject-To with a seller carryback for the equity gap.
- **Low Equity (<20%):** Prime candidates for pure Subject-To deals, potentially with a small cash payment to the seller for their remaining equity or moving costs.
- **Free & Clear (100% Equity):** Exclusively for pure Seller Finance, as there is no existing mortgage to take over.

## 2. Offer Logic Framework: Tiers and Triggers

### Level 1: Subject-To Focused (Low Equity / High Motivation)
- **Trigger Conditions:**
  - Estimated Equity Percent < 20% OR
  - Days on Market > 90 days.
- **Offer Price:** 70% of Asking Price (or lower)
- **Terms:**
  - Down Payment: 0-10%
  - Interest Rate: Take over existing mortgage rate (< 5%) or seller carryback at 0-3%
  - Amortization: 40+ years
  - Balloon: 7-10 years

### Level 2: Market Value Focused
- **Trigger Conditions:**
  - Equity 20-50% AND DOM 30-90 days
- **Offer Price:** 70% of Asking

### Level 3: Seller Finance Focused (High Equity / Free & Clear)
- **Trigger Conditions:**
  - Equity > 50% OR Property is Free & Clear
- **Offer Price:** 100% of Asking
- **Terms:**
  - Down Payment: 0-10%
  - Interest Rate: 0-5%
  - Amortization: 40+ years
  - Balloon: 7-10+ years

## 3. Hybrid Structures

### Scenario A: Existing Mortgage + Significant Equity (20-50%)
- Subject-To: Take over existing mortgage
- Seller Carryback: Finance the equity gap

### Scenario B: Low Equity + High Asking Price
- Subject-To + Small Cash Payment ($10K-$25K)

## 4. Underwriting Considerations

- Due-on-Sale Clause: Acknowledge and mitigate
- Property Condition: Factor repair costs
- Market Rent vs PITI: Ensure positive cash flow
- Exit Strategy: Evaluate refinance, rental, or resale potential

## 4B. Investor & Wholesaler Protections ⭐

### Required Contract Clauses:

**7-Day Inspection Period:**
- Buyer has 7 calendar days to inspect property
- Can terminate for ANY reason during inspection period
- Earnest money fully refundable upon exit

**Assignability (Wholesale Rights):**
- Buyer can assign contract to end investor
- No seller consent required
- Original buyer released after closing

**Contingencies:**
- Financing contingency
- Title contingency  
- Property condition contingency

### Risk Mitigation:
1. Use Third-Party Servicer
2. Title Insurance
3. Property Insurance (buyer as additional insured)
4. Escrow company
5. Attorney review

## 5. Integration with Market Analysis

- **DSCR:** >1.1 for Sub-To, >1.3 for Seller Finance
- **Cash-on-Cash Return:** >15% for Sub-To, >10% for Seller Finance
- **Market Demand:** Validate high-demand areas
