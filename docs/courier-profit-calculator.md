## 📞 Rate Negotiation Tips

### When Client Pushes Back

1. **"Your rate is too high"**
   - Show your cost breakdown
   - Explain what's included
   - Offer tiered pricing options

2. **"We can get it cheaper"**
   - Ask what they're comparing
   - Highlight value (reliability, insurance, tracking)
   - Walk away if below cost

3. **"Can you do it for..."**
   - Never negotiate on the spot
   - Say "Let me review and get back to you"
   - Always recalculate before accepting
